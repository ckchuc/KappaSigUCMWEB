History of Kappa Sigma’s Rho Omicron Chapter

The Kappa Sigma Rho Omicron Chapter was first colonized on May 09, 2009. This initiative was taken due to what was charged by the most important member of the Kappa Sigma fraternity below

“May we not rest contentedly until the Star & Crescent is the pride of every college and university in the land."

Stephen Alonzo Jackson Most Important Member In History, Kappa Sigma.

the founding fathers listed to your right has able to charter the Kappa Sigma Rho Omicron Chapter on February 12, 2011 on the UC Merced campus. With the dedication and the hard work of our founding fathers, the Rho Omicron chapter has given endless opportunities to brothers to become the best version of themselves, whilst also giving back to the local Merced community. We as the current brothers of the Kappa Sigma Rho Omicron chapter are forever greatful for the door you have opened for us. Thank you gentlemen.
