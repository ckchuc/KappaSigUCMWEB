Message from the President.

Hello everyone!

Welcome to the official website of the Rho-Omicron Chapter of the Kappa Sigma Fraternity! Our chapter is based out of one of the newest Universities of the 21st century, the University of California, Merced. I am both excited and honored to serve as the Grand Master and President of this chapter for the upcoming year.

The UC Merced campus is a distinguished research institution with extraordinary potential. The campus administrators and student body have worked diligently to create an open, friendly, and resourceful environment for young leaders to thrive. The Greek community is making extraordinary strides and our Fraternity shares in the benefits and loves to express them upon the general community..

The Kappa Sigma Fraternity recognizes the hidden potential of the University, the Community, and the Students. We have made lasting relationships with the University as our members have become involved in campus activities ranging from research, administration, athletics, and especially education. This organization has affected the Community as our members have volunteered at the local elementary schools, food pantries, general philanthropy events, and much more.

We recognize that every individual has significant potential and we aim to foster the growth of individuals. Our members have dedicated themselves to the principles of Fellowship, Scholarship, Leadership, and Service – the four pillars of the Kappa Sigma Fraternity. We hold our diversity, work ethic, diligence, and commitment to the highest regard as these qualities embody the definition of our brotherhood.

This chapter, in particular, has reaped the rewards from the many leaders which have graduated from this University and Fraternity. Our chapter has earned national awards, broken philanthropic records, and has fostered the growth of members who have founded business and apps, or now work at businesses such as Tesla and Google. We plan on assisting you in reaching your full potential as a leader, a student, and an individual – all while enjoying the ultimate college experience.

“If your actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more, you are a leader.” The Kappa Sigma Fraternity strives to refine leaders that will positively impact the community and invite all who are looking to make a difference to reach out. If you have any questions do not hesitate to message me. We hope to hear from you soon and look forward to helping you become a better you.

Trevor Bailon

Grand Master & President
